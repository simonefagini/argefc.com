ARGE FC

ARGE /ˈaʁɡə/ noun

Abbreviation of Arbeitsgemeinschaft: a working group formed around a shared objective.

ARGE FC is a football club founded in 2021 by a collective of small
architecture offices to compete in the Basel S AM Cup.

As of 2026 is currently formed by Guerra Clauss (founder), an-a-c (founder), Kooperative E45, Studio Hammer, Studio Bosco Ferreira, Diwersy Weimar, Atelier Neume and SUPERFLUO.
