ARGE FC

ARGE /ˈaʁɡə/ noun

Abb. of Arbeitsgemeinschaft: a working group formed around a shared objective.


ARGE FC is a football club founded in 2021 by a collective of small architecture offices to compete in the Basel S AM Cup.

As of 2026 is formed by Guerra Clauss (founder), an-a-c (founder), Kooperative E45, Studio Bosco Ferreira, Diwersy Weimar, office for ordinary objects (ooo), Atelier Neume, Studio Hammer and SUPERFLUO.
